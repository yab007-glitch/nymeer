Introduction

NYMEER ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at nymeer.com.

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website. When you accept cookies, we use Plausible Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. Plausible is privacy-friendly analytics that does not track individual users or sell your data.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of receiving marketing communications

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us through our website's contact form.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
